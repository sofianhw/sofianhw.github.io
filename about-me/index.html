About Me

Jun 11th, 2015 11:00 pm

One of the founders of a startup company: Crazy Hackerz (a.k.a Crackerz) and Inetku an internet service provider company. After setting up Crackerz, Sofian got himself involved in a number of projects requested by big companies to create websites, softwares and applications. In addition, along with Crackerz, sofian created his own products that proved to be successful. One of them is Utees. A web-based platform that specializes on online Tshirt designs.

In the course of his career, Sofian had been an instructor at BinusCenter. Some other accomplishments achieved in his life are attaining the award for The Best Instructor in BinusCenter 2009, winning the Blackberry Jam champion Hackathon Jakarta 2012, and Blackberry Jam champion Hack Asia held in Bangkok in 2012.
